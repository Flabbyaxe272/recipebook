# Nauvoo Syrup

__Original Source__: Madison got it from a friend

__Servings per Recipe__:

__Description__: One of the easiest and sweetest syrups I've had. It's also the thickest.

***

## Ingredients

### Part 1

- 1 c. Butter
- 1 c. Sour Cream (or buttermilk?)
- 2 c. Sugar

### Part 2

- 1 tsp Vanilla
- 1 tsp Baking soda
- 1 tsp Maple flavoring

***

## Instructions

1. Mix Part 1 and bring to boil in a BIG pot for two minutes. (Syrup will foam up when the baking soda is added)
2. Remove from heat. Then add Part 2 to the pot and stir.
