# Pulled Pork

__Original Source__: Unknown

__Servings per Recipe__: 6-8 servings

__Description__: Found this on the Farris Recipe Google Drive. Don't know where it came from, but wanting to try it out sometime.

***

## Ingredients

- 1 pork butt (3-5 lbs)
- 1 can of lemon-lime soda
- 3 Tbsp of salt
- 2 Tbsp of pepper

***

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a deep oven safe pan.
2. Cover and cook in a 275&deg;F oven, until it hits an internal temp of 195&deg;F (approx 3 hrs).
3. Pull it out and let it sit for 30 minutes, then pull it apart. ENJOY!
