# Peanut Butter Squares

__Original Source__: Katie Farris

__Servings per Recipe__: ??

__Description__: Fantastic no-bake peanut butter squares.

***

## Ingredients

- 1 c. extra chunky peanut butter
- 2 c. powdered sugar
- 1 c. butter[^butter], melted
- 6 sheets graham crackers
- 12 oz semisweet chocolate chips, melted

[^butter]: Calls for 2 sticks. So each stick is &frac12; c. of butter, it's 1 c.

***

## Instructions

1. Mix peanut butter, powdered sugar, butter, and graham crackers and press into a baking sheet.
2. Freeze mixture for 30 minutes.
3. When done, take melted chocolate and pour over frozen bars, and freeze again until stiff.
4. Cut and enjoy!
