# Pumpkin Bread

__Original Source__: Grandma JB

__Servings per Recipe__: 4 small loaves

__Description__: A fall favorite! Justin and Rachel make it for General Conference in October. :leaves:

***

## Ingredients

- 1 c. Vegetable oil
- 2 c. white sugar
- 2 c. brown sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 &frac12; tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp nutmeg
- &frac12; tsp cloves
- &frac12; tsp ginger, ground
- 4 tsp baking soda
- 4 eggs
- 2 &frac12; c. pumpkin (equal to 30 oz. can)
- 5 c. flour
- 1 pkg chocolate chips (2 c.), your choice of dark or milk chocolate

***

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients in a mixer.
2. Pour into 4 sprayed small loaf pans, filled only about &frac12; to &frac23; full. (It will rise.)
3. Bake at 350&deg; for 60-70 minutes until it passes the toothpick test.
