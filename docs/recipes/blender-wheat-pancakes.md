# Blender Wheat Pancakes

(with Powdered Milk)

__Original Source__: Unknown

__Servings per Recipe__: 8 pancakes

__Description__: Pancakes made easy in a blender.

***

## Ingredients

- 1 cup water  
- &frac13; cup powdered milk  
- 1 cup uncooked whole wheat  
- 2 eggs  
- 2 tablespoons oil  
- 2 teaspoons baking powder  
- 2 tablespoons honey/sugar  
- &frac12; teaspoon salt  

***

## Instructions

1. Place water, powdered milk, and whole wheat in a blender. Blend on the highest speed for 4 minutes, or until the batter is smooth.  
2. Add eggs, oil, baking powder, honey/sugar, and salt. Blend on low until mixed well.  
3. Bake on a hot griddle.

*Note*: If needed, double the recipe one batch at a time to give your blender a rest.  
*Tip*: If you have whole wheat flour, simply mix 1 cup of whole wheat flour with milk and proceed with the other ingredients.
