# Chicken and Dumplings

__Original Source__: Bonnie Miller, Louisville, OH [^1]

[^1]: From the *Fix-it and Forget-it: Instant Pot Comfort Food Cookbook* by Hope Comerford

__Servings per Recipe__: 4

__Description__:

***

## Ingredients

### Chicken

- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 2 celery ribs, cut into 1" pieces
- 6 small carrots, cut into 1" pieces
- 2 c. Chicken broth
- 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
- 2 tsp chicken bullion
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- 1 tsp poultry seasoning

### Dumplings

- 2 c. buttermilk biscuit mix
- &frac12; c. plus 1 Tbsp milk
- 1 tsp parsley
