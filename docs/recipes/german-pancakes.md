# German Pancakes

__Original Source__: Unknown

__Servings per Recipe__:

__Description__: I don't remember this recipe but knowing that Grandpa Hanson served in Germany, I wouldn't be suprised if this was an actual German recipe.

***

## Ingredients

- 6 eggs  
- 1 cup milk  
- 1 cup flour  
- Pinch of salt  
- 6 tablespoons butter  

***

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 450°F.  
2. Melt the butter in a 9x13 pan.  
3. Blend the remaining ingredients in a blender.  
4. Pour the blended mixture into the pan.  
5. Bake for 15 minutes at 450°F.  
6. Sprinkle with powdered sugar before serving.
