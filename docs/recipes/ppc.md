# Polly's Perfect Chicken

__Alternate Names__: PPC, Chicken Mushroom Gravy over Rice

__Original Source__: Polly and Aunt Barbara

__Servings per Recipe__: 6 servings

__Description__: A family favorite!

***

## Ingredients

- 4 boneless chicken breasts
- 1 pkg zesty italian dry dressing mix
- 1 Tbsp butter
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup
- 8 oz cream cheese
- 1 c. milk
- Egg noodles or rice

***

## Instructions

1. In Crockpot&reg;, cook chicken[^chicken] with butter and dressing mix. Cook on high for 4 hours or on low for 6-8 hours.
2. Cut up the cooked chicken and return it to the Crockpot&reg;.
3. Add cream cheese and cream of mushroom soup. Mix well until combined.
4. Add milk as needed to achieve a thick gravy consistency.
5. Continue to heat in the Crockpot&reg; for another hour.
6. Serve over prepared rice or egg noodles.

[^chicken]: Chicken does not need to be defrosted.
