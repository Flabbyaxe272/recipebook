# Hamburger Rice and Sauce

__Original Source__: Dad Wassom

__Servings per Recipe__: 4 servings

__Description__: From the Wassom Family, a dish that doesn't require much, and is perfect to make your meat go further and fill up a hungry family. For a more balanced, richer flavor, try [Creamy Beef and Rice Skillet](creamy-beef-and-rice.md).

***

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground hamburger
- 2 c. cooked rice
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup
- 1 c. sour cream
- Soy Sauce

***

## Instructions

1. Brown 1 lb ground hamburger in a skillet; drain excess fat.
2. Stir in about 2 cups cooked rice until evenly combined.
3. Add 1 can cream of mushroom soup and about 1 cup sour cream; mix thoroughly.
4. Add soy sauce a little at a time, stirring, until the mixture reaches a fairly tan color and tastes seasoned (you may need more than you expect).
5. Heat gently until warmed through and serve.
