# Dad's Smokey Cheeseball

__Original Source__: Dad Farris

__Servings per Recipe__: 1 Cheeseball

__Description__: Best Smokey cheese ball

***

## Ingredients

- 1 (8 oz) package cream cheese
- 1 tsp garlic powder  
- 1 tsp onion powder  
- &frac14; tsp salt  
- 2 tsp lemon juice  
- 1 chunk each of smoked cheddar & sharp cheddar  
- *For extra heat: add cayenne pepper, or buffalo wing cheese*  

***

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients together. Adjust cheeses to your preferred taste.
2. Chill for 2-4 hours.
3. With gloves on, form into a ball and roll in shredded sharp cheddar.
4. Chill for 1-2 more hours.
5. Serve with wheat thins. ENJOY!
