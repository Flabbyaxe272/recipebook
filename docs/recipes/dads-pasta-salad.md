# Dad's Pasta Salad

__Original Source__: Dad (Keith) Farris

__Servings per Recipe__: 6 servings

__Description__: I remember this as a very good summer lunch or snack, as it was chilled, filling, and very tasty. Mom always made it best. (sorry Dad :sweat_smile:)

***

## Ingredients

- 1 pkg tri-color rotini
- 2 chicken breasts cubed
- Maple jalapeno seasoning
- 6 mozzarella sticks, cubed
- 3 sausages, sliced[^saus]
- 1 italian dressing pkg, dry mix
- Italian dressing[^italdress]

[^saus]: The sausage you want is the mozzarella w/ artichokes & garlic

[^italdress]: Olive Garden brand is the best!

***

## Instructions

1. Cook rotini in boiling water. Drain, place in large bowl, & cool in fridge.[^chill]
2. Cook cubed chicken, season with maple jalapeno seasoning.
3. In separate pan, cook sausages until heated through. Chill chicken & sausages after cooking.
4. Cut cheese sticks into bite-size pieces.
5. Mix cheese stick bites in with noodles, add italian pkg & italian dressing.
6. Add chicken & sausages when cooled.
7. Chill in the fridge for 3 hours. ENJOY!

[^chill]: When draining noodles, you should also spray cold water over the noodles to stop cooking and chill faster.
