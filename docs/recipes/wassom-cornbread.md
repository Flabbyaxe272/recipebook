# White Cornbread

__Original Source__: Camille Swenson

__Servings per Recipe__: 12

__Description__: While there are some thoughts as this is more like cake than cornbread, it's a very tasty dish that pairs very well with [Chipotle Chili](chipotle-chili.md)

***

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 c. sugar
- 3 c. Bisquick
- 3 T. cornmeal
- 3/4 t. baking powder
- 2 1/4 cubes melted butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 c. milk

***

## Instructions

1. Mix together all ingredients.
2. Pour into an ungreased 9 x 13 pan.
3. Bake at 350Â°F for about 30 minutes, until set.

> This is totally rich and so extra good with chili, etc.
