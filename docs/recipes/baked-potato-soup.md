# Baked Potato Soup

__Original Source__: Wassom Cookbook[^1]

[^1]: Contributed By: Lorri Findley, a dear friend

__Servings per Recipe__: 6

__Description__: Great fall (autumn) or winter meal served with rolls!

***

## Ingredients

- 4 large baking potatoes (about 3 lbs)
- &frac23; c. butter
- &frac23; c. flour
- &frac34; tsp. salt
- &frac12; tsp. pepper
- 6 c. milk
- 8 oz. sour cream
- 1 bunch green onions
- 10 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
- 1-2 c. shredded sharp cheddar cheese

***

## Instructions

1. Bake potatoes at 350Â° until done, and then let them cool completely. Easiest way to do this is to bake them the night prior.
2. Peel and cube potatoes, then set aside
<!-- Potentially try to peel, then bake, then cube.-->
1. In large saucepan, melt butter at medium heat. Then stir in flour, salt, and pepper until smooth.
2. Whisk in the milk and increase heat to bring it to a slow boil. Cook and stir until thickened, then remove from heat.
3. Whisk in sour cream and return to medium heat.
4. Add potatoes, green onions, bacon, and cheese.
5. Garnish with more cheese and bacon, and serve hot!
