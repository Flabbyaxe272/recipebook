# Mom's Chili

__Original Source__: Mom (Debbie) Farris

__Servings per Recipe__: ??

__Description__: I know Mom made this often and Dad made a variation that's really spicy for Chili cookoffs by adding Ghost pepper seasoning and letting it just simmer for 12 hours.

***

## Ingredients

- 1 lb hamburger
- Diced onion
- 2 cans tomato sauce
- 1 can beans (pinto or kidney)
- 1 pkg McCormick chili seasoning

***

## Instructions

1. Brown hamburger in a large pot on medium heat. Add diced onion and cook until fragrant.
2. Drain fat until there's about a tsp left.
3. Add tomato sauce, beans and chili seasoning, stirring together while bringing it to a boil.
4. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes before serving with cornbread.
