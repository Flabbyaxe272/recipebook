# Pancakes

__Original Source__: Unknown

__Servings per Recipe__: Unknown

__Description__: Simple and easy pancake recipe. Not as ez as Krusteaz&reg;, but pretty easy.

***

## Ingredients

- 4 c. flour
- 8 Tbsp sugar
- 8 tsp baking powder
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 eggs
- 4 c. milk
- 1 c. canola oil (or Vegetable oil if that's all you have)
- 2 tsp vanilla

***

## Instructions

1. Heat your pan or griddle to medium low heat.
2. Combine all of the dry ingredients and mix together.
3. Add in the liquids and eggs; mix until smooth.
4. Pour the mix onto the pan with a scant &frac13; c.
5. When bubbles form and pop on top, flip it over and cook until golden.
