# Breadstick / Pizza Dough

__Original Source__: Mom (Debbie) Farris

__Servings per Recipe__: 12

__Description__: The original name of this recipe was "Rick's College Breadsticks/Pizza dough" or something like that. Mom picked it up while at college and it's been a family favorite for a quick, tasty breadstick (and pizza dough) recipe ever since.

***

## Ingredients

- 1 Tbsp active dry yeast
- 1 &frac12; c. water
- Honey
- 3 &frac12; c. flour
- &frac34; tsp salt
- &frac14; c. butter (melted)

### Optional

- Garlic seasoning
- Parmesan cheese (grated, like the dusty kind)

***

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400&deg;F.
2. In a bowl or mason jar, warm the water to body temperature and add small amount of honey. Mix in the yeast and let it sit for 1 minute or two, allowing the yeast to activate.
3. In a seperate bowl (preferably a mixer), mix in flour and salt. While mixing, slowly add yeast mixture to combine the dough.
4. Mix until the consistency of bread
5. Roll out breadsticks and bake in a pan for 20 minutes.

Alternatively, you can use the dough as pizza crust for homemade pizzas!
