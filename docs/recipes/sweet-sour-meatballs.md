# Sweet and Sour Meatballs

__Original Source__: [allrecipes](https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/16305/sweet-and-sour-meatballs-ii)

__Servings per Recipe__: 5

__Description__: A delicious sweet and sour sauce is the perfect accompaniment to these meatballs. Serve over rice for a complete meal.

***

## Ingredients

### Meatballs

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 cup dry bread crumbs
- &frac12; cup finely chopped onion
- 1 large egg
- salt and pepper to taste

### Sweet and Sour Sauce

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup water
- &frac12; cup ketchup
- &frac12; cup apple cider vinegar (or to taste)
- 2 Tbsp soy sauce
- 2 Tbsp cornstarch

***

## Instructions

1. Mix beef, bread crumbs, onion, egg, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl until well combined.
2. Shape mixture into 1&frac12;-inch diameter balls.
3. Cook meatballs in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat until evenly browned and cooked through.
4. Mix brown sugar, water, ketchup, vinegar, soy sauce, and cornstarch together in a medium bowl until smooth.
5. Pour sauce over meatballs. Reduce heat to low and simmer until sauce is thick, 3 to 5 minutes.
6. Serve hot and enjoy!
